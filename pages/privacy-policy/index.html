Privacy Policy

How 5 Star Bouncy Castles handles and protects your data.

Who we are

In this policy, 'we' refers to 5 Star Bouncy Castles and its representatives, and 'you' refers to website visitors and customers. Data is handled in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and relevant Irish legislation. The data protection contact and company owner is Jay Barr, contactable on +353 85 131 5822.

What we store and why

When you make an enquiry or place an order we store and process the details you give us, such as your name, contact details and IP address, so we can provide our services as a party and entertainment provider, and for tax and insurance reasons. Only data given directly to us and needed to run the business is stored.

Retention

Personal details may be kept for up to 5 years after you were a customer, for tax and insurance purposes, and are then anonymised unless you opted in to ongoing communications.

Access and sharing

Only authorised staff access your data. Some service providers also hold a copy, for example our website hosting and email providers, and social media platforms if you contact us through them. Data is never sold or given to third parties for marketing. Data may be shared with third parties where required for legal reasons, such as an insurance claim, tax audit or fraud prevention.

Your rights

You can request details of any data stored about you, sent to the email address you provided. You can request deletion, though some data may need to be kept for tax or insurance reasons. You can opt in or out of marketing at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

This website stores temporary cookies to allow enquiries and orders, which cannot identify individual visitors. Third parties, such as analytics providers, may also set cookies under their own policies.

Complaints

The Data Protection Commission (DPC) is the authority for data protection in Ireland and can be contacted for unresolvable problems.
